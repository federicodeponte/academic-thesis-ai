A cornerstone of academic integrity and scholarly credibility is the accurate and reliable citation of sources {cite_018}. The Crafter Agent system places paramount importance on citation discovery accuracy, addressing one of the most significant challenges associated with generative AI: the propensity for hallucination {cite_010}. Unlike general-purpose LLMs that might invent citations or misattribute information, the Crafter Agent employs a sophisticated, API-backed citation discovery mechanism, ensuring that all references are verifiable, relevant, and correctly formatted {cite_003}. This approach stands in stark contrast to the inherent risks of LLM hallucination, where models can generate plausible but non-existent or incorrect bibliographic information, thereby undermining the trustworthiness of the generated content {cite_018}.

The system's methodology for citation discovery involves a multi-layered process. When a claim or piece of information requires evidentiary support, the "Researcher Agent" or a dedicated "Citation Agent" interacts with established, reputable academic databases and APIs {cite_003}. These APIs, such as CrossRef for DOI verification, PubMed for medical literature, or specific university library databases, provide access to vast repositories of peer-reviewed publications and scholarly works.
