## MODERATE ISSUES (Should Address)

### Issue 2: Overclaim Regarding ETS Guarantee
**Location:** Paragraph 3, "gew칛hrleistet gleichzeitig, dass die festgelegte Gesamtemissionsgrenze eingehalten wird" (repeated later in the same paragraph).
**Claim:** The ETS *guarantees* that the overall emission limit is met.
**Problem:** While the theoretical design of a cap-and-trade system aims to limit total emissions, real-world implementation faces challenges (e.g., compliance monitoring, accurate reporting, potential for fraud, political adjustments to the cap, banking/borrowing rules) that can complicate this "guarantee." The term "gew칛hrleistet" is too strong.
**Evidence:** The statement implies a perfect system, which is rarely the case in practice. The subsequent discussion of challenges (e.g., "Cap zu hoch angesetzt wird") already hints at deviations from this ideal.
**Fix:** Hedge the claim. For example, "Das System ist darauf ausgelegt, die Gesamtemissionsgrenze einzuhalten und bietet hierf칲r den Vorteil einer garantierten Obergrenze..." or "theoretisch gew칛hrleistet". Acknowledge that real-world factors can influence the strict adherence to the cap.
**Severity:** 游리 Moderate - an overclaim that doesn't fully reflect the complexities discussed elsewhere in the introduction.
