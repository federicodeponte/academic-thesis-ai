# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining a professional, collaborative, and respectful academic environment for all contributors to this project.

## Standards for Professional Behavior

### Expected Conduct

Contributors are expected to:

- Use respectful and professional language in all communications
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the research community
- Show respect for differing viewpoints and experiences
- Demonstrate empathy toward other community members
- Give proper attribution for ideas, code, and research
- Maintain academic integrity in all contributions

### Unacceptable Conduct

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

- Plagiarism or misrepresentation of others' work
- Deliberate submission of false or misleading information
- Disruptive or disrespectful communication
- Personal attacks or insults
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional academic setting

## Academic Integrity

This project is intended for legitimate academic research purposes. Contributors must:

- Properly cite all sources and references
- Ensure transparency in methodology and data collection
- Report findings honestly, including limitations
- Respect research ethics guidelines
- Follow institutional review board (IRB) requirements where applicable

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior
- Removing, editing, or rejecting contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- Code repositories
- Issue trackers
- Discussion forums
- Email communications
- Social media accounts related to the project

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including:

- Warning the contributor
- Temporary or permanent ban from project participation
- Reporting to relevant academic institutions if necessary

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted for academic research contexts, emphasizing professional collaboration and academic integrity.

## Contact

For questions or to report concerns, please contact the project maintainers through the repository's issue tracker or security policy channels.
