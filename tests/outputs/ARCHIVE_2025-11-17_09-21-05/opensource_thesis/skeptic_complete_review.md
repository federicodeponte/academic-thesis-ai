**Location:** Entire Introduction (Sections 1, 1.1, 1.1.1, 1.2, 1.3, 1.3.1-1.3.5, 1.4)
**Problem:** At 2715 words, this section is far too long for an introduction. An introduction should set the stage, provide a concise overview, and clearly state the paper's thesis and objectives without going into exhaustive detail.

**Fix:** Drastically condense the introduction (aim for 500-800 words).
